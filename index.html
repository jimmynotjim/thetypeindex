The Type Index

Collecting the World’s Resources on Typography

Mastering typography is difficult. Even the most experienced designers will tell you that they are always learning something. The first step, as always, is knowing where to begin. As a self-taught designer and developer, I found that was an incredible barrier to entry. Without instructors or design leads to guide me, I was left to bumble through the web on my own. Luckily I wasn’t detered, and as I searched (and obsessed) about typography, I collected every resources I came across.

The Type Index is a permanant, public home for these resources, available for everyone to access and benefit from. Whether you’re looking for Videos to watch, Books for reference, or Collections that inspire, you can find them here. Ready to get started?
