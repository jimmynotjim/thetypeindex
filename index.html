The Type Index

Collecting the World’s Typographic Resources

Mastering typography is difficult. Even the most successful designers will say they’re always learning something new, but the first step, as always, is knowing where to begin. As a self-taught designer and developer, I found that just knowing where to turn was an incredible barrier to entry. Luckily I wasn’t detered, and as I searched (and obsessed) about typography, I collected every resources I came across.

The Type Index is a permanant, public home for these resources, available for everyone to access and benefit from. Whether you’re looking for Videos to watch, Books for reference, or Collections that inspire, you can find them here. Ready to get started?
